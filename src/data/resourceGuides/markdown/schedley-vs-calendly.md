## The Spam Booking Problem: Where Calendly Comes Up Short
Calendly was designed for a world where your booking link is shared selectively. But in 2026, booking links live in email signatures, websites, social profiles, and LinkedIn bios — making them a magnet for spam bots, fake leads, and unqualified bookings.
### What Calendly Offers for Spam Protection
Calendly has made incremental improvements to spam prevention, including:
- Email verification: invitees receive a 6-digit code to confirm their email before a booking is confirmed
- Single-use links: prevent the same link from being reused
- Secret event types: hidden from your public landing page
- Manual booking limits and advance booking restrictions
- An internal spam monitoring system and Terms of Use enforcement team

However, there are significant limitations. Calendly's community forums are filled with users reporting persistent spam bookings even after enabling email verification. Critically, Calendly still does not offer a blocklist feature to prevent specific people from re-booking, and the email verification only confirms inbox access — not whether the email belongs to a real, qualified prospect.
### What Schedley Offers
Schedley was architected from day one around the premise that not every booking deserves to reach your calendar. Key differentiators include:
- Real-time email validation: checks whether the email address is legitimate and active before the booking completes
- Intelligent spam filtering: pattern recognition on booking behavior, not just email addresses
- Qualification layer: ensures bookings come from qualified contacts, not bots or bad actors
- Calendar clutter elimination: your calendar shows only meetings worth having

## Who Should Use Schedley vs. Calendly?
Choose Schedley If:

## The Core Difference: Reactive vs. Proactive Calendar Protection
Calendly's approach to spam is reactive: it lets bookings happen, then gives you tools to report or clean up afterward. Email verification adds a step, but doesn't evaluate the quality or intent of the booking.
Schedley's approach is proactive: it validates emails in real time and applies intelligent filters before the booking ever confirms. Your calendar stays clean by default, not by exception.
For professionals whose time is their most valuable asset, the difference between reacting to calendar clutter and preventing it entirely is the difference between a productive week and a wasted one.
